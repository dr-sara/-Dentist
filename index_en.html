About Dr. Sarah

Dr. Sarah Aboud is a distinguished dentist with over 6 years of experience in cosmetic and therapeutic dentistry. She earned her Bachelor of Dental Surgery from the National Rabat University (2011-2016) and completed a course in cosmetic dentistry at Dentolern Training Center (2022). She specializes in smile design, dental laser treatments, and modern dental therapies using the latest techniques to provide exceptional care.
